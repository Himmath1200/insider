Principle of Least Privilege (PoLP)

Users should only have the minimum access necessary to perform their job functions.

Implementation Steps:
  1. Define clear roles based on job functions
  2. Assign minimum required permissions to each role
  3. Conduct quarterly access reviews
  4. Remove unnecessary access immediately
  5. Use separation of duties where applicable
  6. Document access decisions and approvals
